Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines what data PawwPrint collects, how it's used, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following user data through user prompts and user activity on PawwPrint:

  • Username
  • Email
  • Location (optional)
  • Bio
  • Password
  • Last login
  • Pet name
  • Pet breed
  • Pet gender
  • Saved pictures
  • Liked pictures
  • Messages
  • Followers
  • Following
  • Want to see
  • Streak
  • Pet profile picture
  • Account creation date

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Filter the Discover Page feed
  • Improve the user experience
  • Perform internal analytics

3. Data Sharing

PawwPrint does not sell, share, or trade your personal information with any third parties.

4. User Rights

Users have the right to request that their data be removed at any time. Please contact us to initiate this process.

5. Tracking and Analytics

PawwPrint does not track its users beyond the data required to provide the platform’s core features.

6. Advertising and Google Ads

In the future, PawwPrint may display Google Ads on our platform. These ads may be personalized based on your usage and interests.

Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site. Google's use of the DoubleClick cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on visits to PawwPrint and other sites.

7. Data Retention

Data is stored for the lifetime of the user account. When an account is deleted, all associated data is permanently removed from our systems.

8. Data Security

We use secure storage and multiple-factor authentication to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9. Age Requirements

Users must be 13 years of age or older to use PawwPrint.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy in the future. All users will be notified of changes via email.

11. Contact Us

Zero Tolerance Policy: Child Abuse and Exploitation

We are committed to providing a safe and respectful environment for all users on our platform. We have a strict zero tolerance policy for any content or behavior that involves child abuse, child exploitation, or any related activities. Our app, a social media platform for pet lovers, is designed to promote joy, companionship, and creativity — and we take the protection of our community, especially minors, extremely seriously.

1. Prohibited Content

Any user found to be posting, sharing, or soliciting the following types of content will be permanently banned and reported to the appropriate authorities:

  • Sexual or suggestive content involving minors
  • Images or videos that depict or imply child abuse or exploitation
  • Grooming behavior or predatory messaging
  • Any form of solicitation involving minors
  • Attempts to obtain private information from minors

2. User Responsibilities

All users are expected to report any suspicious or inappropriate content they encounter. If you come across any content or user behavior that may violate this policy, please use the in-app reporting feature immediately.

To report:

  • Tap the three-dot menu next to a post or user profile
  • Select "Report"
  • Choose the most appropriate reason (e.g., "Child Exploitation")
  • Submit your report for review by our moderation team

Reports can also be made by emailing our safety team directly at efrenllanes17@gmail.com.

3. Enforcement

Our moderation team monitors reports 24/7 and will take immediate action on any content or users found to be in violation of this policy. This includes:

  • Permanent ban of offending accounts
  • Removal of all offending content
  • Notification of and cooperation with law enforcement agencies

4. Protecting Our Community

We use automated tools and human moderation to detect and prevent abuse. We also educate our users on safe interactions and have age restrictions in place to prevent misuse.

By using our platform, you agree to uphold these standards and help keep our community safe for everyone — especially the most vulnerable.
