PawwPrint Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 28, 2025

1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines what data PawwPrint collects, how it's used, and your rights regarding that information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following user data through user prompts and user activity on PawwPrint:

  • Username
  • Email
  • Location (optional)
  • Bio
  • Password
  • Last login
  • Pet name
  • Pet breed
  • Pet gender
  • Saved pictures
  • Liked pictures
  • Messages
  • Followers
  • Following
  • Want to see
  • Streak
  • Pet profile picture
  • Account creation date

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Filter the Discover Page feed
  • Improve the user experience
  • Perform internal analytics

4. Data Sharing

PawwPrint does not share your personal information with any third parties.

5. User Rights

Users have the right to request that their data be removed at any time. Please contact us to initiate this process.

6. Tracking and Analytics

PawwPrint does not track its users outside of what's necessary to provide core functionality.

7. Data Retention

Data is stored for the lifetime of the account. If an account is deleted, all related data will also be removed.

8. Data Security

Data is stored securely using several-factor authentication and encryption methods. However, no method of storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Age Requirements

Users must be 13 years of age or older to use PawwPrint.

10. Policy Updates

Users will be notified by email of any changes to this Privacy Policy.
